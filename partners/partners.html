Our Partners

Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County

The Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County is a premier institution dedicated to inspiring wonder, discovery, and responsibility for the natural and cultural worlds. Through their partnership with BEDChI, they provide expertise in collections management, conservation, and public education.

Their world-class facilities and research staff contribute significantly to the digital preservation and presentation of cultural heritage from the Bears Ears region.

Indigenous Communities

We work closely with indigenous nations and tribal communities who have ancestral connections to the Bears Ears region. Their guidance and traditional knowledge are essential to ensuring respectful and accurate representation of cultural heritage.

All digitization and presentation of cultural materials is conducted with community consultation and approval, honoring tribal sovereignty and cultural protocols.

Academic Institutions

BEDChI collaborates with universities and research institutions to advance best practices in digital cultural heritage preservation. These partnerships support ongoing research, student training, and technological innovation.

Technology Partners

Our technology partners provide expertise in 3D scanning, photogrammetry, augmented reality development, and digital asset management. Their contributions enable us to create high-quality digital representations that serve both preservation and educational goals.
